public enum ScrollbarVisibility

Specifies if/when a scrollbar should be displayed.


Namespace: EV3devKit
Package: ev3devKit

Content:

Enum values:

  • AUTO - The scrollbar is only displayed when the contents are larger than the Scroll area.
  • ALWAYS_HIDE - Never show the scrollbar.
  • ALWAYS_SHOW - Always show the scrollbar.
